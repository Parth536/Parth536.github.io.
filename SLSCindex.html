Our Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1950 by the visionary Mr. Sunderlal Gupta, our company began as a small trading house with a grand vision. Over the decades, under the leadership of Mr. Suresh Chand Gupta, we have grown into a formidable force in the chemical industry.

Our journey is marked by a relentless pursuit of quality, a commitment to our clients, and an unwavering ethical compass. We have adapted to changing markets, embraced innovation, and built lasting relationships that are the bedrock of our success.
